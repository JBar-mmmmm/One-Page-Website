The Australian Game Industry & The Power of Education

For years now companies like Kahoot and Duolingo have taken advantage of gamified learning to aid all kinds of people's education. Gamified education and using video games in education continue to provide opportunities for people to engage with and develop their understanding of all manner of topics.

Over half of parents surveyed in the DA20 said their children have used video games as part of their curriculum and classroom learning.
